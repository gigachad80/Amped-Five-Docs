### 5.3 Deblur Type Selection

| Blur Characteristic | Visual Clue | Best Filter | Key Parameters |
|--------------------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| Linear motion, constant direction | Streaks in one direction | **Motion Deblur** | Angle, Size, Strength |
| Varying motion per frame | Blur changes frame-to-frame | **Variable Motion Deblur** | Keyframe angles/sizes |
| No direction, uniform soft | Overall softness, no streaks | **Optical Deblur** | PSF size, Strength |
| Camera shake + object motion | Complex mixed blur | **Nonlinear Deblur** | Motion model, iterations |
| Heat shimmer, mirage | Wobble, atmospheric | **Turbulence Deblur** | Strength 0.3-0.7 |
| Unknown blur cause | Uncertain origin | **Blind Deconvolution** | Auto-detect mode |
