## 5.4 Sharpening Selection

| Image Condition | Noise Level | Best Filter | Parameters |
|-----------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Clean, minimal noise | Very low | **Laplacian** (acceptable) | Strength 0.5 |
| Any forensic image | Low-high | **Unsharp Masking** (preferred) | Radius 0.5-2.0, Amount 0.5-1.5, Threshold 1-3 |
| Mixed detail distribution | Any | **Adaptive Sharpen** | Strength, adaptation |
| Noisy/compressed | High | **Unsharp Masking ONLY** | High Threshold (2-3) critical |
