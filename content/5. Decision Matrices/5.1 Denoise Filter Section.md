## 5.1 Denoise Filter Selection

| Noise Type | Characteristics | Best Filter | Parameters |
|------------|-----------------|-------------|------------|
| Gaussian noise | Random smooth noise, low-light | **Bilateral Filter** | Space 30, Range 0.2, Iter 2 |
| Salt-pepper noise | Random black/white pixels | **Median Filter** | Kernel 5×5 or 7×7 |
| Compression artifacts | 8×8 or 16×16 blocks | **Deblocking** → Bilateral | Strength 0.7, then Bilateral |
| General degradation | Blur + noise combined | **Wiener Filter** | Auto noise estimate |
| Structured noise | Varies | **Rational Smoothing** | (newer Amped versions) |
