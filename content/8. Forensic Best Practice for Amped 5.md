## 8. FORENSIC BEST PRACTICES

### 8.1 Chain of Custody
1. **Hash original file** immediately after loading (SHA-256)
2. **Document hash** in case file/report
3. **Process with non-destructive workflow** (all filters reversible)
4. **Export enhanced version**
5. **Generate new hash** for enhanced version
6. **Report both hashes** - proves original preserved, enhancement documented

4. **Document what was redacted and why**
